    NUTRITION/DIETICIAN

    What is Nutrition and Dietetics? Dietetics is about healthy eating practices which act as a preventive and curative means and pave the foundation for a healthier life. At Health IQ, we regularly counsel our patients about a nutritious, balanced diet that consists of carbohydrates, fats, proteins, minerals, vitamins and electrolytes for their general well-being. Good nutrition is essential for normal organ development and function, normal reproduction, growth and maintenance of muscles and tissue, optimum activity and working efficiency, building resistance to infection and in repairing body damage or injury. The Nutrition and Dietetics team at Health IQ aims to improve the clinical, biochemical, cellular and psychological status of individuals experiencing complications or side effects of various treatments by providing adequate nutrition and related information.
